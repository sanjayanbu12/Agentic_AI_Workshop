Job Title: Data Analyst Intern
Company: Cognizant
Location: Coimbatore, India
Job Type: Internship, Full-Time (40 hours/week)

Description:
Cognizant is looking for motivated and detail-oriented Data Analyst Interns to join our dynamic digital operations team in our state-of-the-art Coimbatore facility. In this role, you will be at the heart of our data-driven decision-making process. You will work with vast datasets to uncover business insights, build compelling data visualizations, and directly support our project teams with actionable recommendations. This is an exceptional opportunity to gain hands-on experience, learn industry-standard tools, and understand the full lifecycle of data analytics in a leading global technology company.

Eligibility Criteria:
- Currently pursuing a B.E/B.Tech in Computer Science, Information Technology, Statistics, or a related quantitative field.
- Expected to graduate in 2026.
- Strong foundational knowledge of SQL and relational databases.
- Familiarity with a programming language like Python (especially with Pandas/NumPy libraries) is a plus.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.
- Must be authorized to work in India and be able to work from our Coimbatore office.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Prior project experience (academic or personal) involving data analysis or visualization.
- Experience with at least one data visualization tool (e.g., Power BI, Tableau, Google Data Studio).
- Basic understanding of statistical concepts.
- A strong desire to learn and a proactive attitude.

Key Responsibilities:
- Assisting with the end-to-end process of data collection, cleaning, and preprocessing to ensure data integrity.
- Developing, automating, and maintaining interactive dashboards and reports using tools like Power BI or Tableau.
- Performing exploratory data analysis to identify significant trends, patterns, and anomalies.
- Collaborating with senior analysts and project managers to understand requirements and present findings clearly.
- Documenting data sources, methodologies, and results.

Benefits:
- Competitive monthly stipend.
- Structured mentorship program with experienced data professionals.
- Potential for a full-time offer (Pre-Placement Offer - PPO) upon successful completion of the internship.
- Full access to Cognizant's extensive internal training and industry certification programs.
- Networking opportunities with company leaders and fellow interns.

Interview Process:
1. Online Assessment: A timed test covering logical reasoning, quantitative aptitude, and basic technical skills (SQL, Python).
2. Technical Interview: A one-on-one interview with a senior analyst to discuss your technical projects, and test your problem-solving and SQL skills.
3. HR Interview: A final round to assess your cultural fit, communication skills, and passion for a career in data.

Company Culture:
At Cognizant, we are a high-performing team that values collaboration, innovation, and continuous learning. We foster an inclusive environment where diverse perspectives are celebrated. As an intern, you are considered a full member of the team, and your contributions are valued from day one.

Application Deadline: December 15, 2025

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with web technologies (e.g., HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React).
